Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a campaign?

Click on the "Start Your Campaign" button on our homepage and follow the prompts to create your campaign.

How can I make a donation?

Browse active campaigns on our platform, choose the one you'd like to support, and click "Donate Now." Follow the secure payment process to complete your donation.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption to ensure all transactions are secure.
